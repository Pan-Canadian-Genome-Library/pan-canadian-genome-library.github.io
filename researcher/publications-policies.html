Indigenous Engagement & Data Sovereignty​

  • A First Nations Guide to the Privacy Act. First Nations Information Governance Centre. 2024. English. French.
  • Circumpolar Inuit Protocols for Equitable and Ethical Engagement. Inuit Circumpolar Council. 2022. English.
  • DNA on loan: issues to consider when carrying out genetic research with aboriginal families and communities. Laura Arbour, Doris Cook. Community Genetics, 2006. English.
  • Indigenous Data Sovereignty Toolkit. Native Women's Association of Canada (NWAC). 2026. English. French.
  • Indigenous peoples and inclusion in clinical and genomic research: Understanding the history and navigating contemporary engagement. Angela Waanders, Alex Brown, Nadine R. Caron, Alexa Plisiewicz, Sean T. McHugh, Thinh Q. Nguyen, Kaitlin Lehmann, Jeffrey Stevens, Phillip J. Storm, Adam Resnick, Tom Belle Davidson, Sabine Mueller, Cassie Kline. Neoplasia, 2023. English.
  • Inuit Governance Practices. Connie Strayer, Robyn Grebliunas. BCcampus, 2024. English.
  • National Inuit Strategy on Research. Inuit Tapiriit Kanatami. 2018. English. French. Inuktut Qaliujaaqpait. Inuktitut Syllabics (ᐃᓄᒃᑎᑐᑦ ᖃᓂᐅᔮᖅᐸᐃᑦ).
  • Promoting self-determined Indigenous data governance in Canada: the Métis Health Research and Data Governance Principles. Robert Henry, Chelsea Gabel, Caroline Tait, Kiera Kowalski, Alexandra Nychuk. Health Promotion International, 2026. English.
  • Practical Application of an Indigenous Research Framework and Two Qualitative Indigenous Research Methods: Sharing Circles and Anishnaabe Symbol-Based Reflection. Lynn F. Lavallée. International Journal of Qualitative Methods, 2009. English.
  • Two-eyed seeing: a framework for understanding indigenous and non-indigenous approaches to indigenous health research. Debbie H. Martin. Canadian Journal of Nursing Research Archive, 2012. English.
  • Unbecoming Claims: Pedagogies of Refusal in Qualitative Research. Eve Tuck and K. Wayne Yang. Qualitative Inquiry, 2014. English.

Equity, Diversity, & Inclusion​

  • Including multiracial individuals is crucial for race, ethnicity and ancestry frameworks in genetics and genomics. Daphne O. Martschenko, Hannah Wand, Jennifer L. Young, Genevieve L. Wojcik. Nature Genetics, 2023. English.
