# Consent Template to Support Indigenous Data Sovereignty
The PCGL and the [Silent Genomes Project Indigenous Background Variant Library (IBVL)](https://www.bcchr.ca/silent-genomes-project/ibvl) have partnered to provide Indigenous individuals and communities participating in research with the option to have their data held under Indigenous protection, governance, and oversight. Together, they developed a **Consent Template to Support Indigenous Data Sovereignty** that is now available for use by the [Canadian Precision Health Initiative (CPHI)](https://genomecanada.ca/challenge-areas/canadian-precision-health-initiative/) projects, as well as any other research projects planning to submit data to the PCGL.
